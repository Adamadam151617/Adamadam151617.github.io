Rollback Netcode and why its cool

Rollback Netcode is a network structure that handles lag in pear to pear conections by reusing the remote users inputs to avoid slowdown, then when the missed packages arive double checks if our "prediction" is correct and if not we resimulate from the missed frame with the correct input information
